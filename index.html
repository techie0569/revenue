Market Opportunity in India

Market Definitions

  • Total Addressable Market (TAM)

    The total potential market if every eligible student adopted our platform. Our target is students from Tier-2, 3, and 4 cities, a population of ~120-130 million students.

  • Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)

    The portion of the TAM we can realistically serve initially, representing a target of 10% of our TAM, or 12-13 million students.

  • Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)

    Our realistic target market in the first 3–5 years, projected at ~1 million students.
